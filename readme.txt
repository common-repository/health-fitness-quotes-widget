Generates a random quote from a list of 500+ health and fitness quotes on every page load.

== Description ==
This widget will show a random health and fitness quote in your sidebar or footer on every page load. We are open to suggestions and will keep improving this plug-in. The quotes shown in this plugin are said by famous personalities, health experts, nutrionists and gym instructors. In the current version of health and fitness plugin there are more than 500 quotes.
It can be used on health and fitness websites to motivate the readers.
** About us: **
We have a gym for females and specialize in providing guidance for [females to lose weight](thefitterfemale.com) in [Lexington](https://thefitterfemale.com/lexington-free-trial/) and [Acton](https://thefitterfemale.com/acton-free-trial/).

== Installation ==
1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/plugin-name` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the Health and Fitness Quotes through the \'Plugins\' screen in WordPress
3. Now go to Appearance -> Widgets and add \'Health and Fitness\' widget in your sidebar or Footer.
4. Give the widget a title of your choice.
